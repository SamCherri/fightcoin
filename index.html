About the Project

Husky FC is a visionary football club, built from scratch by a community of supporters. It begins with the sale of unlimited Fan NFTs, granting symbolic recognition and a future exclusive role in our Discord community.

No profit promises. Total transparency. A football club born from the blockchain.
