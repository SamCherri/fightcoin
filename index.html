About the Project

Husky FC is the world's first football club created and funded entirely by a community through cryptocurrency and NFTs. Built on the Shibarium network, it's a decentralized, transparent, and inclusive movement. The club starts with its own stadium and is governed by DAO voting — fans are in control.

🎟️ NFT Supporter Access

Support the project by minting your NFT. Founding NFTs unlock exclusive access and future privileges within the community. All verification is done manually during the initial phase.

🗺️ Roadmap

  • Phase 1: Community building, website launch, NFT supporter release – US$0
  • Phase 2: Token deployment on Shibarium, locking mechanism until soft cap – US$20,000
  • Phase 3: Marketing push, team building, stadium concept draft – US$50,000
  • Phase 4: Investment in physical and digital infrastructure, DAO voting system – US$100,000

💰 Token: SFC

SFC is the official token of Husky FC, launched on Shibarium. It will be locked for transfers/sales until the minimum capital is raised. Community-governed and publicly transparent, funds will be distributed as follows:

  • 60% – Football infrastructure and operations
  • 10% – Marketing and promotion
  • 10% – Team and development
  • 10% – Operational costs
  • 10% – Emergency reserve

🌐 Community & Access

Holders of NFTs or tokens will have access to exclusive voting, perks and governance rights. Telegram and Discord integration will follow — join us and shape football history.
